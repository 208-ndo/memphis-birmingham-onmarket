# Email Contact Policy

1. verified_agent_email = eligible for first live email test after manual review.
2. verified_brokerage_email = not eligible for first live email test unless manually approved as brokerage/office outreach.
3. contact_form_only = no Gmail send; use contact form manually or build separate form workflow later.
4. phone_only = no Gmail send.
5. Never guess or infer emails.
6. Do not copy found_email into review_queue.json unless contact_status is verified_agent_email, or unless a human manually approves brokerage outreach.
