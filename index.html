KISS Quick Reference
Owner Finance Rules
Band: $30k–$80k
Offer: FULL list price
Down: 5% (covers agent commission at close)
Balance: 100 monthly payments @ 0%
Earnest: $500  |  Close: 21 days
Due Diligence: 10 days, as-is
Your fee: 12% buyer down – 5% your down
Agent: 6% of purchase + flat fee
Cash KISS Tiers
$80k–$150k → 40% of value
$150k–$300k → 50% of value
$300k–$750k → 60% of value
$750k–$1.5M → 65% of value
Agent: 6% of cash offer + flat fee
Assign at: $10k+ above contract
Close: 7–14 days, as-is, no contingencies
Pitch Must Hold
At-list baseline = 3% of list price (buyer-side only)
Your total to agent must be ≥ 3% of list
Owner Finance: 6% commission + flat fee ≥ 3%
Cash: 6% of offer + flat fee ≥ 3% of ARV
If pitch fails: do not send the offer
Pitch fails rarely — sub-$50k with tiny flat fee
Email Hard Rules
Subject (OF): neutral — never "seller financing"
Subject (cash): can say "cash offer"
Length: 3–5 sentences max
Em dashes: NEVER — use commas/periods
Commission: exact dollar amount always
CTA: soft — just ask where to send offer
