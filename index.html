Cash As-Is Offer — 229 Holdings LLC
AI Email — 4 Variations
Cash offer framing. Speed + certainty + commission. Rotate before sending.
Cash Lowball Formula
MAO = (ARV × 90%) − (Repairs × 2) − Fee
90% ARV: your absolute ceiling
2× repairs: rehab + holding/closing baked in
Fee: start $30k, walk down $10k at a time
Contract price: MAO − your assignment fee
Assign at: MAO = what buyer pays you
Cash Deal Rules
Agent: 6% of contract price + flat fee
Pitch must hold: total ≥ 3% of ARV
Close: 7–14 days, as-is, no contingencies
Earnest: $500
Inspection: 10 days
Assignment: buyer reserves right to assign
Fee Walk-Down Logic
Start at $30k assignment fee
If offer goes negative: walk to $20k
Still tight: walk to $10k
Under $10k: pass on the deal
Never go below: $10k minimum fee
Buyer needs: 20%+ ROI to move
Email Hard Rules
